Online Accounting Lite

Online Accounting Lite is a hosted invoicing application. The project was started because I felt that many individuals and small businesses just need a app that can keep track of their clients, invoices & quotes as well as track payments. Many people can't afford expensive accounting packages like QuickBooks & Sage Pastel. Hence I started creating Online Accounting!

Online Accounting Lite is available for free to anyone who needs a basic self-hosted accounting system.

Features Include
  • Full Dashboard
  • Client Portal
  • Clients
  • Inventory
  • Quotes
  • Invoices
  • Online Payments
  • Reports
  • Custom Fields
  • Tax | VAT Calculation
  • Tasks & Projects
  • Fully Customizable
  • Your Custom Branding
  • No limitations | Free Forever
